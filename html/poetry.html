      I wanna just hold you close and forget the world:
      You see, I want that interconnected kind of love
      That infectious, kind of love;
      Running through my veins there's no pain
      I cannot regret this kind of love
      Let alone turn down your embrace for it is strong, and delectable; I yearn to respect this kind of love
      I want that quick to forget everything that upset you, kind of love
      That undeniable, regrettably inconceivable; kind of love
      The kind you can never expect to forget because it is always feasible, kind of love
      That tangible, releasable, and seasonal; kind of love
      That revolving telekinetic and always teachable, kind of love
      That...peaceful, kind of love
      That reaches out and grabs you not out of disparity but because it is a piece of you, kind of love
      That I will never defeat you, kind of love
      The kind of love where you already anticipate my passion for you so that I never have to speak again. 

      Inspiration:
      Rudy Francisco-
      Love Poem Medley Extended @Cal Poly San Luis Obispo
